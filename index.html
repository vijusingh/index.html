Sushant Singh Rajput

1986 - 2020

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor known for his remarkable performances in television and film. He began his career with TV shows, gaining recognition for his role in "Pavitra Rishta," and transitioned to films with his debut in "Kai Po Che!" His film career includes memorable roles in "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story" and "Chhichhore." Sushant's untimely death in 2020 left a void in the entertainment world, but his legacy continues to inspire millions.

Notable Achievements

  • Breakthrough role in "Kai Po Che!" (2013)
  • Famous portrayal of MS Dhoni in "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story" (2016)
  • Awarded for "Chhichhore" for Best Film (2019)
